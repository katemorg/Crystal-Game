Crystals Collector!

How to Play:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below, and the value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it. These values change at the start of each new round.

By clicking on a crystal you will add the hidden number of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
